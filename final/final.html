Our Mission

Here at University Colorado Boulder we have Disability services to support students, faculty, and staff with accomodations and more! Disability Services is dedicated to providing students with disabilities an equal opportunity to participate in university programs, courses and activities through reasonable accommodations and services. Our office is here to support students, staff and faculty with accommodation requests, implementation, guidance and general information.
